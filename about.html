About

My name is Carlos and I’m a UX/UI designer living in Rio de Janeiro. I’ve been working with UI design for the last 5 years across several projects and on different design agencies.  I’m a graphic design graduated on a tradicional and well-know Brazilian university.

Although my love for graphic and UI/UX I consider myself just a “designer”. I have been delivering brand identity, brochure, poster, book covers, presentations, t-shirts, as web interfaces, social media layout, wireframes, mobile UI and responsive layout. I’m motivated to always learn more. While I’m not working, I’m illustrating, you can see some here.

Had the pleasure to work (and learn) with these teams (as an employee or a freelancer)
